Cyber Safety for Refugee Youth in Canada

1. What Is Cybercrime?

  • Phishing emails or texts
  • Scam job offers asking for ID or money
  • Fake immigration threats
  • Online harassment or hate speech

2. Protect Yourself Online

  • Use strong passwords (12+ characters)
  • Never share personal info online
  • Turn on 2-step verification
  • Log out of public computers
  • Use websites with https://

3. Know Your Rights

You have the right to digital safety and privacy, regardless of your immigration status. You can report abuse or scams safely.
